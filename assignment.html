Swords to Ploughshares

A concept created in the bible which was very popular after WW2. It's a metaphor and a call to action. The mataphor and call both describe the act of taking efforts to make weapons, and converting them to efforts to make life easier, or more prosperous.

There are several popular statues that were created after WW2 to memorialize the importance of this pivot.
